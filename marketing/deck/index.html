01Problem

Community access is fragmented.
Monetization is manual.

Members

Too many separate memberships

Every community means another payment to manage. Cost adds up, value gets hard to compare, and most people just stop joining.

Community managers

No clean way to get paid

Packaging benefits and splitting partner revenue runs on spreadsheets and trust. Manual reconciliation, every single month.

Project owners

Traction is unverifiable

Screenshots and self-reported numbers don't convince partners, ecosystems, or judges. There's no shared proof of activity.

02Solution

One subscription. Automatic split.
Proof on-chain.

Pay once

Bundled access

Connect a Stellar wallet and pay one subscription. It opens benefits across whitelisted partner communities.

Split automatically

Soroban does the payouts

Every payment splits between project owner, community manager, and platform by contract logic. Zero manual accounting.

Verify anything

On-chain traction dashboard

Subscribers, volume, and payout events read straight from the chain. Anyone can check the numbers are real.

04Core features

Four features, one financial loop

7.1

Unified subscription

Connect a supported wallet, pay one subscription on testnet, and the entitlement record updates in the app after confirmation.

7.2

Partner listing layer

Partner resources listed with metadata and subscriber discounts. Tokenization-compatible by design, marketplace comes later.

7.3

Automated disbursement

Fixed-percentage split runs on every successful payment. Payout state is auditable and config changes are access-controlled.

7.4

Traction dashboard

Subscriber count, payment volume, and payout events from testnet data. Judges can match every number to real activity.

05Why Stellar

The right rails for consumer payments

Settlement

Fast and near-free

Recurring consumer payments need instant settlement at negligible fees. That's Stellar's home turf.

Soroban

Programmable money logic

Subscription records, entitlement checks, and the revenue split live in one Rust contract, not in a backend spreadsheet.

Horizon

Public, verifiable reads

The traction dashboard reads the chain directly. Metrics are checkable by anyone, not self-reported.

Wallets Kit

Non-crypto-native friendly

One guided connect-and-pay flow. A first-time user never sees raw keys or manual transaction building.

06Business model

Members get

Communities get

How Komunify earns
